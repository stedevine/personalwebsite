Turton Devine

Before Lori and I got married, we decided that we wanted to have the same last name. I don't like hyphenating names because, from an engineering point of view, it doesn't scale. It also doesn't seem fair that the lady-half of the couple should always give up her name and take her husband's, but I didn't want to give up my family name either. What to do?

We dug around in our family trees and looked for a name that got erased by a past marriage. We found my father's mother's maiden name: Devine.

This seemed suitable so we called ourselves that.
